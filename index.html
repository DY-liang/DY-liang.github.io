I am a visiting researcher at UC San Diego, advised by Xiaolong Wang. I also pursue a Master's in Machine Learning at DTU, advised by Ole Winther.

I was recently a visiting student at UC Berkeley and a research intern at Berkeley AI Research, where I was fortunate to work with Lerrel Pinto and Xiaolong Wang in Alyosha Efros' group. I have also been at Nanyang Technological University (NTUsg), Retune-DSP and raffle.ai.

Research interest

I am broadly interested in research on the generalization and adaptation of intelligent systems. I believe AI in the future should be flexible, learn with little supervision, and learn continously over their lifetime. I work with machine learning, robotics, and computer vision.
